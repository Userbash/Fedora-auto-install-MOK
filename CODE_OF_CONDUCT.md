# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

MOK is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all participants. We value the contributions of all community members and expect everyone to be respectful and professional.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behaviors

We encourage:

- ✅ Using welcoming and inclusive language
- ✅ Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- ✅ Giving and receiving constructive criticism gracefully
- ✅ Focusing on what is best for the community
- ✅ Showing empathy towards other community members
- ✅ Helping others learn and improve
- ✅ Sharing knowledge freely
- ✅ Celebrating successes of community members

### Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- ❌ Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- ❌ Insulting or derogatory comments
- ❌ Personal or political attacks
- ❌ Public or private harassment
- ❌ Publishing others' private information without consent
- ❌ Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in professional settings
- ❌ Trolling or intentionally disrupting discussions
- ❌ Spam or promotional content

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

1. **Clarifying** standards of acceptable behavior
2. **Enforcement** of this Code of Conduct
3. **Taking action** against behavior that violates this Code
4. **Providing support** to affected community members

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness a violation of this Code of Conduct:

1. **Contact**: Email the project maintainers
   ```
   conduct@[maintainer-email-domain]
   ```

2. **Include**:
   - Description of the violation
   - Who was involved
   - When and where it occurred
   - Any relevant evidence (links, messages, etc.)

3. **Privacy**: Your report will be handled confidentially

### Investigation Process

1. **Acknowledgment** - We will confirm receipt within 48 hours
2. **Investigation** - We will interview all parties involved
3. **Determination** - We will determine if a violation occurred
4. **Action** - We will take appropriate corrective action
5. **Appeal** - You may appeal the decision

### Consequences

Depending on severity, consequences may include:

- **First Offense**: Warning and request to modify behavior
- **Second Offense**: Temporary suspension from community
- **Severe/Repeated**: Permanent removal from project

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- ✅ GitHub issues and pull request discussions
- ✅ Commit messages and code comments
- ✅ Project documentation
- ✅ Official project communication channels
- ✅ Community meetings and events
- ✅ Any content representing the project

It does NOT apply to:

- ❌ Private conversations between individuals
- ❌ Other projects or organizations
- ❌ Third-party platforms
